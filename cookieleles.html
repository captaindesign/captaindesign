Cookieleles are
sustainable instruments

Cookieleles are maybe the project where all my quests connects. Design, music, lutherie and environmental concern. Since our specie left the trees we have developed objects and tools to make our life easier.

In post industrial times, we have been losing our capacity to build things. Our technical aspect is now covered by industries wich mostly never takes care about the real needs of the consumer, the environment and the energy wasted to build things that we often discard after a few uses. Buy, use, discard.

I imagine cookieleles as an enduring object wich will connect you with hand-build process, learning, and of course music playing at the same time it teach us how to be conscious consumers.
