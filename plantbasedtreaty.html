Plant Based Treaty

I had the luck to work on the branding of the Plant Based Treaty. A collective agreement where individuals, governments, organizations, and business aligned their energies to replace the old production systems to a more sustainable one. A Plant Based Production System is an urgent need in the middle of a brutal climate change, a massive extinction of species and biodiversity loss which is leading us to a chaotic near future.
For more information visit http://plantbasedtreaty.org
